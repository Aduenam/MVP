About Us

Hello, and welcome!

Aduenam is a web app serving to bring awareness to the immigration crisis, raise money for children separated from their families, and provide everyone access to current immigration news and policies at the convenience of their cell phones.

It was created by two high school girls who want to help immigrants in the U.S and hope to see a change in the ways they are treated.
